Delivery Policy

Shiprocket Shipping

We partner with Shiprocket for reliable delivery across India. All orders are shipped through their network of courier partners.

1. Processing Time

  • Orders are processed within 24-48 hours of confirmation
  • Orders placed on weekends or holidays are processed on the next business day
  • Customized or bulk orders may require additional processing time

2. Delivery Timeframe

  • Standard Delivery: 5-7 business days
  • Express Delivery: 2-3 business days (additional charges apply)
  • Remote Areas: 7-10 business days
  • Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on location and courier performance

3. Shipping Charges

  • Free shipping on orders above ₹500
  • Standard shipping: ₹49 for orders below ₹500
  • Express shipping: ₹99 (2-3 days)
  • Shipping charges are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

  • Order confirmation email with tracking number
  • WhatsApp notification with tracking link
  • Real-time tracking updates via Shiprocket tracking portal

Track Your Order

You can track your order through:

  • Tracking link sent via WhatsApp
  • Order section in your account
  • Shiprocket website with your tracking number

5. Delivery Partners

Our orders are delivered through various Shiprocket partner couriers including:

  • Delhivery
  • Ekart
  • Bluedart
  • FedEx
  • India Post

6. Delivery Address

  • Please provide complete and accurate delivery address
  • We are not responsible for delivery failures due to incorrect addresses
  • Courier will attempt delivery 3 times before returning the package
  • Please provide a mobile number for delivery coordination

7. Delivery Status

Order statuses and their meanings:

  • Order Placed: Order received and confirmed
  • Processing: Order being prepared for shipment
  • Shipped: Order dispatched from our warehouse
  • In Transit: Order on the way to delivery address
  • Out for Delivery: Package with local courier for delivery
  • Delivered: Package delivered successfully

8. Damaged or Lost Packages

  • Please inspect the package upon delivery
  • Report any damage within 24 hours of delivery
  • Take photos of damaged packaging and products
  • Contact customer support with your order number and photos
  • We will arrange for replacement or refund

9. Undeliverable Packages

Packages may be returned to us if:

  • Delivery address is incorrect or incomplete
  • Recipient not available at delivery address
  • Refusal to accept delivery
  • Package damaged during transit

10. Delivery Areas

We deliver across India including:

  • All major cities (Delhi, Mumbai, Bangalore, Chennai, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Pune)
  • All state capitals and tier-2 cities
  • Selected pin codes in rural areas

Note: Some remote locations may have limited delivery options.
